‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No, account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, your leaderboard data, scores, and account information cannot be recovered.

How do I delete local game data?

Local game data (statistics, preferences) is stored on your device. To delete this data, uninstall the Tappy Tac Toe app or clear app data in your Android settings.

What happens to my Google account?

Deleting your Tappy Tac Toe account does not affect your Google account. We only remove the association between your Google account and our app.
